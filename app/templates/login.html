Tracking a Different Order?

This application tracks orders for the currently logged-in Tesla account. To track an order for a different user:

  1. Log out of your Tesla account in your browser (or use Incognito mode).
  2. Click "Open Tesla Login" above.
  3. Sign in with the new account credentials.
  4. Paste the new callback URL here.

Note: Logging in with a new account will overwrite the existing session tokens.
